What is HTML5?

HTML5 is the latest version of the Hypertext Markup Language used to structure content on the web. It introduces semantic elements that make content easier to understand for both users and search engines.

Why Accessibility & SEO Matter

Accessibility for Everyone

Accessibility ensures that websites are usable by people with disabilities. Using semantic HTML5 tags helps assistive technologies, such as screen readers, interpret your content correctly.

SEO for Discoverability

Search engines rely on structured, readable content to rank pages. Proper use of heading tags, descriptive content, and semantic layout improves your site's visibility.
